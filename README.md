# Email Campaign Optimization with Causal Inference

## Overview

This project evaluates the causal impact of email marketing campaigns using randomized experimental data and modern causal inference methods.

Completed as part of the MSc Marketing Science programme at University College London (UCL).

## Business Problem

The objective is to estimate the causal effect of different email campaigns on customer spending and identify which customers should be targeted to maximize marketing ROI.

## Techniques

### Experimental Design
- Randomized Controlled Trial (RCT)
- Balance Check
- Standardized Mean Difference (SMD)
- Love Plot

### Causal Inference
- Average Treatment Effect (ATE)
- Ordinary Least Squares (OLS)
- Causal Forest
- Heterogeneous Treatment Effects (HTE)

### Policy Optimization
- Uplift Modeling
- Customer Targeting Strategy
- Variable Importance Analysis
- Lift Curve
